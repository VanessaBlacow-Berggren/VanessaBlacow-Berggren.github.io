Video Art

Expectations of Filming Cat Videos, phone video, premiere, 2019

Video art project showing the struggles of making a cute cat video of your own cat who refuses to cooperate.

A Relaxing Afternoon Outdoors - Indoors, phone video, photoshop, premiere, 2019

Example of a video installation where people can relax and talk to each other in an outdoor setting, watching the clouds and the day pass by. It would show a full day/night cycle sped up to pass within a few hours.

Net Art

The Link - Puzzle Set 1

html, 2019

A project in critical interactivity where the user has to find their way through the puzzles to continue on to the next page.

The Link - Puzzle Set 2

html, 2019

For the final project I took the critical interactivity to the next level, now there are no instructions or hints for the user to follow. The goal is for them to get inside my head and figure out what I was thinking about when creating the puzzle.
